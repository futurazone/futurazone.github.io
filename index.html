Sakkara Loop

Precision strategy, lightning-fast reflexes, and a hypnotic shower of gems.

Are you ready for a challenge that will hook you from the very first shot? Inspired by classic games but with a unique mechanic, Sakkara Loop forces you to think fast. Every shot counts!

Aim your launcher to create groups of the same color and make them explode in a cascade of points. Don't have the color you need? Shoot another gem to instantly swap your pointer! Use this powerful mechanic to set up master plays and dominate the board.

Features You'll Love:

  • Addictive arcade gameplay: easy to learn, a true challenge to master.
  • One-touch controls: intuitive and precise.
  • Master explosive Power-Ups like the Lightning, the Clock, and the mighty Effigy.
  • Compete in global, national, and historical rankings.
  • Vibrant graphics and satisfying visual effects.
  • Play anywhere: no internet connection needed!
