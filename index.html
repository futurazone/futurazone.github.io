Sakkara Loop

Pixel-perfect strategy, heart-stopping reflexes, and a mesmerizing gem shower!

Are you ready for a challenge that combines pinpoint strategy, heart-stopping reflexes, and a mesmerizing shower of gems? Sakkara Loop is the new arcade puzzle that will hook you from the very first shot!

Inspired by the great classics but with a unique mechanic, Sakkara Loop forces you to think fast. Every shot counts! The board fills up, speed increases... how long can you last?
